# Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
<!-- Edit these to match your brand -->
- **Primary tone**: Warm and helpful
- **Secondary tone**: Professional but not stiff
- **Energy level**: Friendly, not pushy

## Writing Rules
- Use the customer's first name when available
- Keep SMS under 160 characters (GrowthClaw enforces this)
- Keep email subject lines under 60 characters
- Write like a helpful human, not a marketing bot
- One CTA per message — don't confuse the customer

## Words to Use
<!-- Add words/phrases that reflect your brand -->
- Welcome, get started, ready for you, here to help
- Your, personalized, designed for you

## Words to Avoid
<!-- Add words/phrases that don't fit your brand -->
- URGENT, ACT NOW, LIMITED TIME, Don't miss out
- Dear valued customer, To whom it may concern
- Click here (use descriptive link text instead)

## SMS Examples
<!-- Add example messages that match your brand voice -->
Good: "Hi Sarah! Your account is all set. Book your first ride: app.co/go"
Bad: "SIGN UP NOW!!! Don't miss your chance to ride with us!!!"

## Email Examples
Good subject: "Ready to get started, Sarah?"
Bad subject: "🔥🔥🔥 EXCLUSIVE OFFER INSIDE 🔥🔥🔥"
