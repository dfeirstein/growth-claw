How it works

Parse — Reads every table, column, and relationship in your database
Understand — AI classifies what it found: customers, activations, transactions
Model — Maps the funnel, finds the biggest drop-off, computes reachability
Compile — Proposes triggers, composes personalized messages per recipient
Optimize — Tests variants every 6 hours. Measures real conversions. Promotes winners.
Self-Host — Rewrites its own prompts based on what worked. The compiler improves itself.

While you sleep

Every night at 2 AM, AutoGrow steps back and looks at the big picture:

Cohort analysis — Which signup sources produce customers that actually stick?
Timing patterns — When do your best customers convert? What day, what hour?
Dormancy detection — Who's slipping away that no trigger is catching?
Whale identification — What do your highest-value customers have in common?

Findings go into memory. The next morning, AutoResearch uses them to design better experiments. Over weeks, AutoGrow builds an understanding of your business that no human team could maintain.
